Delete your data

To delete your PinPark data, follow the steps below inside the app. Your data will be permanently removed upon confirmation.

  1. 1 Open the PinPark app and tap Settings
  2. 2 Tap Account
  3. 3 Tap Account Info
  4. 4 Tap Delete my data
  5. 5 Your saved parking data will be permanently deleted immediately upon confirmation
⚠️ This action cannot be undone. Once deleted, your saved parking information cannot be recovered.

If you're having trouble, please contact support.
