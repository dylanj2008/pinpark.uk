Delete your account

To delete your PinPark account, you can either do it here, or directly through the app. All user data is deleted within 90 days.

To permanently delete your account and all associated data:

1. Click the 'Delete my account' button at the bottom of the box
2. When prompted with the confirmation message, click 'Yes'

Once your account is deleted, your authentication data and saved parking information will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

If you cannot access the app, please contact support.
