Delete your account

Your PinPark account can only be deleted from inside the app.

To permanently delete your account and all associated data:

1. Open the PinPark app.
2. Go to Settings.
3. Tap Delete Account.
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Once your account is deleted, your authentication data and saved parking information will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

If you cannot access the app, please contact support.
